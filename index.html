We need more dinosaurs

Instructions

Jurassic Park needs more dinosaurs in their park. On the right you have four dinosaur buttons, each time you click one a certain number of that dinosaur will be released into the park. You'll have to figure out how many of each type are released to make sure you don't go over the park's dinosaur limit.

It's important we keep the dinosaur numbers under control.

Each time you hit the exact number of dinosaurs needed, your wins will increase. Conversely, each time you go over and release too many dinosaurs, your losses increase.

When the game ends, a new amount will be assigned to each dinosaur and the dinosaur limit will change. Good luck explorers.
